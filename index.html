Ardian Dwi Cahyo
UI/UX Designer & Illustrator
Undergraduate Information Systems Student at Universitas Pembangunan Nasional "Veteran" Jakarta
I’m a final-year student and UI/UX Designer with a strong passion for product and user experience design. My focus is on creating intuitive and adaptive designs that align with both user needs and business objectives.
Over the past years, I’ve deepened my expertise in user experience design through mentoring activities, organizational projects as a Product Designer, and winning a UI/UX Design competition in Surabaya. My experience as a UI/UX Designer Intern at the Ministry of Finance of the Republic of Indonesia further strengthened my ability to craft usable solutions that positively impact both users and organizations.
With the spirit of a Lifelong Learner, I’m committed to continuously growing as a professional Designer and contributing meaningfully through design.
