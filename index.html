Who's Ardian?
Dream to becoming a UI/UX Designer drives him to continuously learn and seek the best experiences in the UI/UX Design field. The role of a UI/UX Designer not only makes him care about the issues faced by society but also motivates him to create innovative products that have a positive impact, both for society and for business sustainability. His experience as a mentor and winning the UI/UX Design competition further strengthened his passion to contribute in a tangible way as a student who has an impact on the surrounding environment.
In this field, he has a strong interest in continuing to develop his potential to become a professional UI/UX Designer, with a focus on accessibility, inclusivity, and innovation that meets the needs of society and companies. With strong skills and ambition, he is committed to bringing bright ideas that can bridge society with technological advancements through meaningful and effective design.
